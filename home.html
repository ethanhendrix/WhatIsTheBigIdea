About Us

Get to know us a bit

Who We Are:

JEP Industrial Brands (‘JEPIB’) is a proudly South African company, with a level 1 BBBEE status due to its black female ownership. JEP IB is an owner managed business which prides itself on exceptional service delivery. We specialise in the distribution of Power tools, Hand tools, Accessories, Personal Protective Equipment -PPE and Corporate Uniforms.

Our Vision:

Transparency and fairness are major drivers for our team, and open honest communication ensures that the journey we embark on together remains exciting and sustainable for all parties concerned. JEP IB’s vision is to grow into a market leader offering the best quality products to our clients and to maintain an honest and ethical relationship in the pursuit of success. Excellence and prompt service is our unique differentiator.

Our Mission:

JEPIB’s mission is to consistently add value to our clients by providing the best quality products and services at the best rates possible. Our credibility is highly important to us and we refuse to supply inferior products to our clients. While we provide fast and reliable services, our quality will not be compromised. We constantly strive and endeavour to exceed expectations in quality, delivery, and cost through continuous innovation, improvement and customer interaction.

Our Promise:

If we don’t have a product that you are looking for, we will source it for you. The business is built on honesty, ethics and professionalism. The future of this business is dependent on commitment and dedication and should be known for its quality of service, products, honesty, efficiency and team work.
